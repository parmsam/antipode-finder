What is an antipode?

Your antipode is the point on Earth's surface directly opposite you — if you could tunnel straight through the center of the planet, you'd pop out there. For most people on land, their antipode is in the middle of the ocean!

Spain and New Zealand are famous near-antipodal land masses. Only about 15% of the Earth's surface has land on both antipodal ends.
